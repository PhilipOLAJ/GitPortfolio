The Best Movies According to Philip.

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Abattoir - By Mount Zion Films

This is my favourite christian movies of all times. The movies is well plotted and set to depict a contemporary world of filled with vices and how the christian experience can be a solution to this world of evils and decadence.

Queen of the South - By M.A Fortin and Joshua Miller

This movies is a season film. It tells the story of a strength in weakness. A lady rises from her weaknesses and little-ness to become the lord of the greatest drug cartel in the south. This movie has scenes shots in almost all the continents of the world. It is full of action, thrills, intrigues and suspence.

Jagunjagun - By Femi Adebayo

A pure Yoruba thriller that shakes the box office to re-write the glory of Africans in the global films terrain. It displays the wrong use of power and influences and the consequences of it. It reminds humanity that power is temporary. This is a movie to watch for anyone who loves African stories.
